The hour of La Récréation rang!

An exotic restaurant, completely atypical in an old school between the two wars. Classroom, playground with courtyard transformed into a lush garden, where one lunch under the chestnut tree or the mulberry trees.

In the evening, lighting illuminates the ancient stone walls of this idyllic and historic place where artists come to exhibit. An inventive cuisine in the spirit of the site, rich in flavors and surprises from all over the world guaranteeing fresh vegetables from the vegetable garden adjacent to the restaurant. The restaurant organizes regular cooking classes and develops a catering service at home.


More than a good restaurant, it's a trip.

The restaurant is located in the town of Arques, a small village located in the south of France in the department of Lot region midi pyrenees. At 26 km from Cahors and 26 km from Gourdon. In the south of the perigord and south-east of the Dordogne The village is classified for the church Saint Laurent the Frescoes of Saint Andrée, the former dean and its tower.
Les Arques is also known for its museum devoted to Ossip Zadkine, the workshops of Les Arques which bring every year a dozen artists from all over Europe, and of course the restaurant La Récréation. What an excitement in such a small village of 217 inhabitants.
