OUR KITCHEN COURSES

Our chef offers cooking classes for a morning and a lunchtime lunch. Come and learn to cook exotic flavors or traditional dishes revisited by our chef Rémy Cougnac during these 3 hours cooking classes. Create with us our ravioli of coral lobster sauce, crispy Saint Jacques with the hair of angels, supremes of boneless quails or even a mango sweet heart flowing passion. You will attend a cocktail creation that will allow you to accompany your gourmet creations of the noon.

Prices for cooking classes:

76 €: cooking lesson of 2h30 + cocktail creation + lunch
40 €: accompanying lunch.
