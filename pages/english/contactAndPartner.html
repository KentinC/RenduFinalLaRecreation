OUR PARTNERS

Wanting to respect our region and highlight it, we work with local partners that allow quality products.
Our summer vegetables or our flavored herbs come from the vegetable garden next door! A vegetable garden maintained by a former student of the school who gives heart to the work and therefore of the heart to our vegetables.
For our meat or fish? They are directly chosen by the manager of the establishment so as to respect our commitment to local or French producers!
To finish on a good note, our wine list will offer you a great diversity to accompany all our dishes. Wines from regions such as the tasty Château Lamartine and its three declensions.
